== Description ==

= Publish your own Journal! =

Create immediatly your online journal as a Wordpress spin-off. Choose a layout and publish your articles in a ready to print newspaper-like template. 
 

= Features =
With few and simple steps you can: 

* Assign a name to your journal
* Choose a layout for the frontpage 
* Drag and drop your posts to the chosen frontpage
* Create a summary and populate internal pages
* Publish!

When your digital journal is ready, his aspect is independent from your WP theme and your content will be enjoyed by all your readers!
